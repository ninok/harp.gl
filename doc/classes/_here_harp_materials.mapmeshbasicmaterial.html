Class MapMeshBasicMaterial

Subclass of [[THREE.MeshBasicMaterial]]. Adds new properties required for fadeNear and fadeFar. In addition to the new properties (which update their respective uniforms), it is also required to update the material in their objects onBeforeRender and [[OnAfterRender]] calls, where their flag transparent is set and the internal fadeNear/fadeFar values are updated to world space distances.

see

[[Tile#addRenderHelper]]
